Introduction

Know The Real History Of World Sufi Center

The World Sufi Center, also known as the International Sufi Organization was launched on 30 July 2017 at the closing ceremony of the Ulama International Conference at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia.

The World Sufi Center was launched by the Deputy Prime Minister, Dato' Seri Dr Ahmad Zahid Hamidi following the need for world scholars to gather to plan a da'wah strategy for the people of the end times.

Through it, various international level programs have been successful such as International Naqshbandi Festival, World Salam Day, World Sama' Fest and many more since 2017 until now.
